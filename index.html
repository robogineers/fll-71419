The Innovation Robot Strategy

The Root Problem

"Wasting time in the wrong areas"

Why We Chose This

After interviewing Dr. James McGrath-Seegmiller, we identified that the biggest failure in archaeology is blind digging. Identification by guesswork triggers a chain reaction of dangerous and costly issues.

Artifact Damage

Guessing where to dig means heavy machinery hits fragile history before it is ever mapped.

Safety: Tunnel Collapse

Undetected voids pose extreme safety risks. Ground Truth tech "sees" these before the digging starts.

Environmental Impact

Knowing exactly where to dig minimizes the human footprint on the local ecosystem.

The Innovation Stack

LiDAR Scanner

Laser pulses strip away tree canopies to create 3D terrain models, exposing ruins hidden by the jungle floor.

Dual GPR

Ground Penetrating Radar finds buried walls and artifacts by sensing soil density changes non-invasively.

GIS Mapping

Coordinate mapping syncs all data to a global grid, allowing digital site documentation in real-time.

AI Detection

On-board machine learning identifies outlines of human structures and artifacts automatically.
